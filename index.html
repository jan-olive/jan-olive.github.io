Portable Full-Sized Tournament Chess Board


A few years ago, I 3D printed a tournament chessboard to save money, however a major problem it had was the lack of portability due to its large slab-like nature. To combat this, I designed and printed a tileable board inspired by jigsaw puzzles where each square snaps to adjacent squares which then allow me to transport this board in a bag.
